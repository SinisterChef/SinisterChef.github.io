Wright.edu Homepage Redesign

Using analytic and user testing data, I shifted the focus of the homepage to a single audience - perspective students. In a collaborative effort with development and marketing teams, I created a design that pushes the best qualities Wright State has to offer to its 1.5 million unique annual visitors.

Science Olympiad

Over 2,200 middle and high school students from around the world came to Wright State’s Nutter Convention Center for the 2017 Science Olympiad. Working with the single flag wordmark I was given and a 45 day timeline, I designed the entire aeronautical theme for the event and created all digital media (marquee ads, projection screens, lower thirds, animated scoreboard media, and more).
