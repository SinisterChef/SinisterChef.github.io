Creating and Organizing

I've been making these badges for the past three years. We currently have over 70 different badges that I maintain and keep track of.

Processes

Keeping track of the awards and making sure they are up to date and correct is the largest hurdle when making the badges. Since we didn't have a DAM to utlize I had to figure other means of organization. In Drupal I created a custom taxonomy just for the badges. I then created a custom teams channel for quick review turn around that also functions revision history to keep track of when changes where made or badges first created.
