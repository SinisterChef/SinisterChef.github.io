Creating and Organizing

Since starting the award badge project in 2020, over 70 different badges have been developed. I create, maintain, and update all badges and associated source data.

Processes

Keeping track of the awards and making sure they are current and accurate is the largest hurdle when making these badges. Since we didn't have a DAM to utlize, I had to figure other means of organization. In Drupal, I created a custom taxonomy specific for this project. I then created a custom Teams channel for quick review turnaround that also functions as revision history to keep track of when changes were made or badges were first created.
